Who we are

Welcome to Sidey Electrical. We Specialise in domestic, industrial and commercial electrical services including intruder alarm systems and CCTV. We provide a professional and friendly service with customer satisfaction and quality as a priority.

Where we cover

We are based in Sheffield covering Dronfield, Chesterfield, Nottingham, and the surrounding areas. No matter where you are, we will do our utmost to provide our services.

Certifications & Qualifications

We are members of NAPIT and registered Trust Mark Contractor. We have City & Guilds, NVQ Level 3 Electrical Installation, BS7671 17th Edition Wiring Regulations. We have over 10 years of experience in the installation of a wide range of electrical services to meet all your needs including:

  • Full & Partial Rewires
  • New installations
  • Extensions/Kitchens
  • Consumer Unit Upgrades
  • Sockets, Switches
  • Periodic Inspection and Testing
  • Interior and Exterior Lighting
  • LED Low Energy Lighting
  • Emergency Lighting
  • Fire Alarms
  • Wired & Wireless Intruder Alarms
  • Networking
  • PAT Testing
  • CCTV
  • Door Access
  • Home automation
